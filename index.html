Welcome to the Global Room, the heart of King Chat! This is a borderless space where users from all walks of life gather to connect, share ideas, and hang out. Whether you're here to make new friends, discuss trending topics, or just enjoy a casual conversation, this is the place to be. Step in, say hello, and join our growing community!

Room Rules
  • 1Mutual Respect: Treat every member with dignity. Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • 2No Spamming: Avoid flooding the chat with repetitive messages, excessive emojis, or unsolicited links. Keep the flow natural.
  • 3Privacy First: For your safety, do not share personal information (such as phone numbers, addresses, or passwords) in the public room.
